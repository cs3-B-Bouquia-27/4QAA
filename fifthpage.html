Applications

It's obvious how material science is applied in our daily lives. All of the things we use are a result of material science. Mobile devices, utensils, plates, beds, bags, fans...almost every 'thing' around us is a result of material science. Without it, we'd have struggles in our everyday activities. Thus, proving how material science is a part of our past, present and future as material scientists will continue in striving for discovering new kinds of materials.
